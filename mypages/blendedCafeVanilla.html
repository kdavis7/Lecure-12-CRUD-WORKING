BLENDED CAFE VANILLA RECIPE
Brew time: 8 minutes | Yield: Makes one cup
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of Brewed Hot Coffee
  • 1 cup of cold milk
  • 4 tablespoons of Vanilla Syrup
  • 1 Cup and 1 straw
  • 1 Cup of Ice
  • 1 canister of Whipped cream
  • 1 Blender
Steps:
  • 1. Brew Hot Coffee for Drink.
  • 2. Combine beverage ingredients: Vanilla, Coffee, Milk, and Ice into blender
  • 3. Blend until Smooth consistency.
  • 4. Pour contents into cup and top with whipped cream and straw.
  • 5. Add more sugar as needed and reblend as needed.
