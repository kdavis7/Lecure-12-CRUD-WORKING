Coffee Name Definition
Iced Coffee Hot coffee is brewed over ice to make Iced Coffee.
Hot Coffee Coffee beans are ground, placed in a filter, and brewed in a coffee maker to make Hot Coffee.
Americano An espresso-based drink that is made with espresso shots and hot water.
Blended Coffees Blended drinks made with coffee, typically they include other syrup flavors to go with the coffee and look like a milkshake.
Cafe Misto This drink is made with half a cup of hot coffee and half a cup of steamed cream.
Cappuccino This espresso-based beverage is known for it's frothy consistency and has more foam as the steamed cream is frothed longer to achieve the foamy consistency.
Dalgona Coffee Also known as whipped coffee. This is made with hot water, sugar, and instant coffee. The goal is to stir the coffee until it is a whipped consistency and put it on your favorite drink.
Vietnamese Coffee Made with Vietnamese Coffee beans. It is brewed hot coffee that uses the drip brewing method and is mixed with sweetened condensed milk.
