AMERICANO RECIPE
Brew time: 7 minutes | Yield: Makes one cup
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup of Hot coffee
  • 1/2 cup of Hot Water
  • 1 Ceramic Cup and 1 spoon
  • 1 tablespoon of cream (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar(optional)
Steps:
  • 1. Brew Coffee.
  • 2. Combine Water and Coffee into the cup.
  • 3. Mix together with a spoon.
  • 4. Add sugar or cream as needed.
