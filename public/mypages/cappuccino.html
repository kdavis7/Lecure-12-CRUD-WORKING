CAPPUCCINO RECIPE
Brew time: 13 minutes | Yield: Makes one cup
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup of Hot coffee
  • 1/4 cup of Steamed Cream
  • 1 Ceramic Cup and 1 spoon
  • 1 hand-held electric frother
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar(optional)
Steps:
  • 1. Brew Coffee.
  • 2. Steam a cup of cream in the microwave or on the stove.
  • 3. Mix brewed coffee and steamed cream in coffee cup.
  • 4. Add sugar as needed.
  • 5. Use the electric frother to whip up the foam in the cup.
