DALGONA RECIPE
Brew time: 10 minutes | Yield: Makes one cup
Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons of Hot Water
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Instant Coffee
  • 1 Ceramic Cup and 1 spoon
Steps:
  • 1. Combine Hot water, sugar, and instant coffee mix into a cup.
  • 2. Take a spoon and stir until it is a whipped consistency.
  • 3. Scoop out the whipped coffee and put on your favorite drink.
  • 4. Add sugar as needed.
